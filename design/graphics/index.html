Drawing the Line

POSTER, March 2017

Designed for the organization Overcome the Gap to promote an event at McMaster University. The event encouraged a discussion regarding the topic of free speech and where the line on politically correct speech must be drawn.

DSIR

COVER, May 2015

Designed for The Doon School Information Review, a student-run magazine for which I served as Editor-in-Chief in high school. The magazine contains articles and reviews about pop culture. This issues theme was political awareness, in light of the then-recent shooting at the offices of Charlie Hebdo in Paris, France. The issue was dedicated to the journalists at Charlie Hebdo, and in memory of RK Laxman, a prominent Indian cartoonist who often used comedy to express his political views.
The issue can be found atissuu.com/abhayrajjain/docs/issuu
